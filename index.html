For the fig-swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add broen sugar and stir to dissolve.Halve all of the figs and toss in the saucepan woth water and lemon fuice. Cool over mediun heat, stirring frequently, untill you have a chunky-jammy mixture. add salt woth one or two stirs, set aside and let cool completely

Ice cream: In a small pot over medium heat, combine milk, and granulated sugar until sugar is completely dissolved and the mikl is just bareky lukewarm. Whisk in the egg yolks. Set mixture in the fridge and wait until the fig mixture is cooled.

Using an ice cream machine, pour liquids into the frozen basin and ptocess according to manufacturer instuctions,i.e., let spin and thicken for 20 minutes before adding mascarpone, fig jam mixture, and the nuts. Continue to process for +/- 10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for at least two hours before serving.
